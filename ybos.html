Summary of Young-Baxter Operators (YBOs)

Young-Baxter Operators (YBOs) are crucial in areas like quantum groups, knot theory, and statistical mechanics. They satisfy the Yang-Baxter equation:

R₁R₂R₁ = R₂R₁R₂

This equation governs the interaction between three particles or states in a system. Below, you can explore specific solutions for 4x4 and 9x9 YBOs matrices.
