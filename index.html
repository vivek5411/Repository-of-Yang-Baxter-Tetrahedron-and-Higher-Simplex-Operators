About Yang-Baxter operators

Yang-Baxter and 2-simplex operators play a key role in both Physics and Mathematics, with applications in quantum systems, knot theory, and quantum computation. Their higher-dimensional counterparts, n-simplex operators, extend these uses and are gaining importance. This website offers a comprehensive update on the latest advancements in this field..
